# Newsletter form with Risor

Capture newsletter signups with a Hula form, validate via captcha, and fan out to a third-party newsletter service via a Risor hook. Includes the form schema, the captcha integration, and the hook source for forwarding to Mailchimp / Buttondown / your-API.
